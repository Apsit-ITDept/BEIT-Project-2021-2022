Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sichai Yojna

Eligibility Criteria

1. Farmers of any category, category are eligible to avail this scheme.
2. It is mandatory for all the applicant farmers to have their own agricultural land.
3. In this scheme, members of Self Help Groups, Trusts, Cooperative Societies, Incorporated Companies, Producer Farmers’ Groups are eligible to take benefit by registering.
4. Farmers who have been cultivating the land under the Lease Agreement for the last seven years will also be able to take advantage of the PM Krishi Irrigation Scheme.
5. Only citizens (farmer brothers) residing in India are eligible to take advantage of this Prime Minister Agricultural Irrigation Scheme.

Benefits

Subsidy on purchase of equipment for irrigation

How to apply

Step 1- Visit the Official Website Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchayee Yojana i.e. http://pmksy.gov.in/.
Step 2- On Homepage, login on the homepage of the website. You can login through your name and email id.
Step 3- After this, you can get all the information related to the Agricultural Irrigation Scheme by going to the About section in the website.

Documents Required

1. Applicant’s Aadhar Card
2. identity card
3. Housing Certificate
4. Farm papers
5. Bank Account Passbook
6. mobile number
7. Passport Size Photo

How to check BeneFiciary List

1.Go to the website www.pmkisan.gov.in
2. Look for the ‘Farmer Corner’ on the website.
3. Click on the ‘Beneficiary List’.
4. Enter your state, district, sub-district, block and village details.
5. After filling this, click on Get Report and get the complete list.
