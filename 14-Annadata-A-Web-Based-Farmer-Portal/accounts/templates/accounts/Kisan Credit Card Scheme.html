Kisan Credit Card Scheme

Eligibilty Criteria -

The farmers who apply for KCC have to ensure that they qualify a few eligibility criteria in order to apply for KCC. Here is the list of eligibility for KCC. Ensure that you fall under any one of the following categories.
1.The farmer applying for KCC should reside or live in the same jurisdiction as that of the given bank.
2. Farmers who are individuals or joint borrowers of the land on which cultivation happens.

Benefits

This scheme extends loan facility to the farmers at interest rates as low as 2% and a collateral-free loan up to ₹1.60 lakh besides a complimentary insurance coverage.

Application Procedure

1. Visit the official site of any bank of your choice and visit the Kisan Credit Card Section.
2. Download the application form and print it out.
3. Fill out the application form.
4. Visit the nearest branch of the bank and submit the application long with the documents asked.
5. The loan officer will provide the details necessary to the applicant.
6. Upon the sanction of the loan amount, the KCC will be dispatched.

Documents Required

Identity proof option


PAN card
Aadhaar card
Driver’s license
Passport
Voter’s ID
Person of Indian Origin Card
Job card issued under Mahatma Gandhi Rural Employment Guarantee Act (MNREGA), and
Letters issued by the UIDAI.

Address proof options:


PAN card
Aadhaar card
Driver’s license
Passport
Voter’s ID
Utility Bill from the preceding three months
Ration card
Property registration document
Person of Indian Origin Card
Job card issued under Mahatma Gandhi Rural Employment Guarantee Act (MNREGA), and
