Care to be taken while spraying pesticides

Care to be taken while using pesticides and herbicides in the field

1.Farmers spray various chemicals to control pests, diseases or weeds in the field. There are three main risks involved in spraying these chemicals.
2Extremely fine particles of chemical drugs enter the body through the airways.
3 While spraying, they enter the body through skin contact and through eyes.
4 While spraying, they can inadvertently enter the body while eating or drinking beedi.

Symptoms and remedies for poisoning

1.Poisoning occurs when pesticides / herbicides come in contact with or ingest them.
2Symptoms of poisoning and other illnesses can often be similar.

Symptoms of poisoning

1Weakness and dizziness.
2Skin irritation, blemishes, sweating.
3 Eye irritation, watery eyes, blurred vision
4 Headache, malaise, muscle aches, tongue twitching, fainting, shortness of breath, chest pain, coughing.

First aid to be given immediately after poisoning

1If pesticides / herbicides get in the eyes, immediately rinse the eyes with clean water for 5 minutes.
2If it is blown on the body, wash it with soap for 10 minutes and take it to the hospital.
3 After poisoning, the patient should be induced to vomit only if he is completely cleansed, otherwise he should not do so.
4 If poisonous drug is spilled on the clothes, change the clothes immediately and take the patient to the hospital as soon as possible.
