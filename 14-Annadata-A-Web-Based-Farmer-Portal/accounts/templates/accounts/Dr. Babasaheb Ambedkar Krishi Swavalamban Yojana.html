Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Krishi Swavalamban Yojana

To create sustainable irrigation facilities for Scheduled Caste / Neo-Buddhist farmers in the State.

Beneficiary Eligibility -

1. It is mandatory for the beneficiary to be from Scheduled Caste / Neo -Buddhist category.
2. The annual income of the farmer by all means should not exceed Rs.150,000 / -.
3. Farmers should have a minimum area of ​​0.40 hectare in order to take advantage of the new well
4. If there is an actual well on the beneficiary 's 7/12 as well as in the field, the new well cannot be availed.
5.There should be no other well within 500 feet of the new well.

Grants to farmers belonging to Scheduled Castes / Neo-Buddhist category -

1. New Well - Rs.250,000 / -s.
2. Repair of old well - Rs. 50,000 / -akhs
3. Inwell boring - Rs.20000/-
4.Micro Irrigation Set - Drip Irrigation Set Rs. 50,000 / -, Sprinkler Irrigation Set - Rs. 25,000 / -
5. Pump set (Diesel / Electricity) - Rs.20000 / -

Documents Required

1. 7/12 excerpt or 8 a. (Original copy)
2. 8 a
3. Aadhar Card
4. Incident Punchnama (Self attested copy)
5. Proof of income from Tehsildar.
6. In case of new wells, proof of availability of water from ground water survey and development system is required.
7. Caste certificate.

How to apply:

1.Application should be made online at www.agriwell.mahaonline.gov.in .
2. Generally available for a period of one month in August / September each year
