Soyabean

Soybean called as golden beans is belongs to legume family. It is native of East Asia. It is a rich source of Protein also excellent source of fibre. Oil extracted from soybean contain small amount of saturated fat. In Punjab, it plays an important role in crop diversification.

Climate

Soyabean needs about 15 to 320C temperature for germination but for rapid growth the crop needs higher temperature. The crop requires about 60-65 cm annual rainfall drought at flowering or just before flowering results in flower and pod drops, while rains during maturity impairs the grain quality of soyabean. harmful. The best soil type is sandy loam having good organic matter content.

Soil

And loamy well drained fertile soil is good for growing soybean. Land preparation for soybean should be done by proper ploughing followed by laddering.

Land preparation

Prepared field by giving two to three ploughings followed by planking.
